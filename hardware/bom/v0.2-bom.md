*Alternative: Hongfa HF46F/12-HS1 (low-profile, 7.5mm height) or HF49FD/012-1H1 (9.0mm, very compact).*
*For lowest height: Omron G6D-1A-ASI (SMD, 5.2mm, 3A) if current allows.*

**Note**: Wayne prefers small package. The SMI series is significantly more compact than standard SRD relays while still handling 10A at 250VAC. For a really tight enclosure, consider Omron G6D (SMD, but only 3A — fine for relay coils but not high-current loads).
